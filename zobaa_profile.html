Biography

Prof. Ahmed F. Zobaa (IEEE Senior Member) is Reader in Electrical and Power Engineering at Brunel University London. A Cairo University graduate (BSc 1992, MSc 1997, PhD 2002), he earned a DSc from Brunel (2017). With 30+ years of academic experience across Egypt, UK, and Spain, he has authored 374+ publications and supervised 30+ PhD students. His research spans power quality, renewable energy, smart grids, and engineering education.
